About Us

About Delight English School

Delight English School is committed to providing a holistic and disciplined learning environment for children from ECD to Grade 10 (SEE), preparing them academically and personally for a brighter future.

Dear Parents, Guardians, and Students,

Delight English School ma tapai haru sabai lai samadar swagat chha. Hami kebal pustakiya gyan ma matra bishwas nagari bidyarthi ko sarwangin bikas (holistic development) ma dhyan dinchhau.

Academic Programs

What We Teach

From early learning to board exam preparation, our curriculum is designed to build strong foundations and future-ready skills.

Early Childhood Development (ECD)

  • Montessori-inspired play-way method
  • Social skills development
  • Communication skills
  • Behavioral learning
  • Motor skills development

Primary & Lower Secondary (Grade 1-8)

  • Government curriculum
  • Advanced English medium materials
  • Creative writing
  • Mental mathematics
  • Science experiments
  • Basic computer education

Secondary Level (Grade 9-10)

  • SEE Preparation
  • Daily and weekly mock tests
  • Extra coaching classes
  • Career counseling
  • Personality development workshops
